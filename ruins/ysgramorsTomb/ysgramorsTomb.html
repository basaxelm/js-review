Ysgramor's Tomb

Ysgramor's Tomb is a tomb found on a frozen island northwest of Winterhold. Most of the tomb is only accessible during and after the The Companions quest "Glory of the Dead." There is a Word Wall for the Animal Allegiance shout located above the tomb.
